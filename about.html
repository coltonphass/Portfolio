About Me

I'm a software engineer who loves problem-solving and learning how things work through trial and error. I started making webpages in 2019, teaching myself HTML and CSS through personal projects and Udemy courses. In 2023, I discovered C++ through school, and that's when programming really became a passion for me. It pushed me deeper into core fundamentals and expanded my interest into languages like Python.

My curiosity has led me to explore various areas of software development, including web development, data structures, algorithms, and AI/ML. I enjoy taking on challenges that push me to grow and improve my skills, and I've built strong connections through my Artificial Intelligence research group and college peers.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy playing video games and practicing martial arts. I've been training in karate and having a lot of fun with it, and I'm also passionate about learning how to create my own video games and interactive experiences—right now my focus is UEFN.
